Happy Birthday sound
====================

Add your music file here as:

  happy-birthday.mp3

Optional second format (some browsers):
  happy-birthday.ogg

The song starts when you tap "एक थिचो, हजार मुस्कान ✨".
Use a short clip or full song — it loops until you tap "रोक्नुहोस्".

Where to get audio:
  - Record your own message
  - Use royalty-free music sites (check license)
  - Export from your music app as MP3
